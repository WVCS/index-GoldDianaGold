    Overview

    Parasite is a movie that follows a poor family known as the Kims. The Kims are going through a financial struggle until
    Ki-Woo, the Kims son, gets an offer from a friend to take over his tutoring job for a rich family. With Ki-Woo being paid well
    the Kims devise a plan to infiltrate the family and rise from the poverty they've known all their life.
